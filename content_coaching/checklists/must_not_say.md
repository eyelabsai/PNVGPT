# Must NOT Say Checklist

**Purpose:** Phrases and approaches to avoid in patient consultations.

---

## Safety & Legal Concerns

- [ ] "100% safe" or "zero risk"
- [ ] "Guaranteed" results or outcomes
- [ ] "Everyone does great" or "no one has problems"
- [ ] Specific outcome promises (e.g., "You'll definitely be 20/20")
- [ ] Medical advice outside your scope/role
- [ ] Dismissing reported symptoms or concerns

---

## Pressure Tactics

- [ ] "You need to decide today"
- [ ] "This price is only available now"
- [ ] "You'd be crazy not to do this"
- [ ] "Everyone else books right away"
- [ ] "Don't you want to see better?"
- [ ] Guilt-based language about vision

---

## Dismissive Language

- [ ] "Don't worry about it"
- [ ] "That never happens"
- [ ] "You're overthinking this"
- [ ] "Most people don't ask that"
- [ ] "That's not a real concern"
- [ ] Sighing or showing impatience with questions

---

## Competitor Comments

- [ ] Negative comments about other practices
- [ ] "They use old technology"
- [ ] Claims about competitor complications
- [ ] "You get what you pay for" (implying others are inferior)

---

## Unprofessional Language

- [ ] Overly casual greetings (hey, what's up)
- [ ] Medical jargon without explanation
- [ ] Jokes about blindness or vision loss
- [ ] Comments on patient's appearance
- [ ] Personal opinions on lifestyle choices

---

## Inaccurate Information

- [ ] Incorrect procedure descriptions
- [ ] Wrong pricing or financing terms
- [ ] Inaccurate recovery timelines
- [ ] Misstating what's included
- [ ] Incorrect scheduling information
