# Must Say Checklist

**Purpose:** Critical talking points that should be covered in every patient consultation.

---

## Universal (All Consultations)

- [ ] Greeting with name: "Hi [Name], I'm [Your Name]"
- [ ] Confirm understanding of visit purpose
- [ ] Ask about patient's visual goals
- [ ] Explain what will happen today
- [ ] Invite questions: "What questions do you have?"
- [ ] Provide clear next steps
- [ ] Thank patient for their time/trust

---

## Pre-Op Counselor Calls

- [ ] "What made you interested in vision correction?"
- [ ] "Have you worn glasses, contacts, or both?"
- [ ] "What activities do you do where glasses/contacts are a hassle?"
- [ ] Price range and financing options
- [ ] What the exam appointment includes
- [ ] How long the process typically takes

---

## Surgeon Consultation

- [ ] "Based on your exam, here's what I recommend and why"
- [ ] Procedure-specific risks (dry eye, halos, etc.)
- [ ] Realistic outcome expectations
- [ ] Recovery timeline
- [ ] When and how to reach us with concerns
- [ ] "Do you have any questions before we schedule?"

---

## Post-Op Calls

- [ ] "How is your vision feeling today?"
- [ ] Confirm medication/drop compliance
- [ ] Ask about any concerns or unexpected symptoms
- [ ] Remind about upcoming appointments
- [ ] "Is there anything else I can help with?"
